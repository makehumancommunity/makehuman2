<h1>Change material of the Basemesh</h1>

This context provides a possibility to change the material (skin) of the base mesh.

<p>
<h3>Filtering</h3>

To select a skin material, you can use a filter with some predefined values, like gender, era and genre.
As long as the materials are tagged with these attributes, they will be shown.
Materials with no attributes are always shown. You can:
<ul>
<li>select from the attributes</li>
<li>click on a filter icon</li>
<li>or enter a text in the filter box</li>
</ul>
